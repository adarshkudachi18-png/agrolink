Design a mobile app UI for a farmer-to-retailer marketplace platform called “AgroLink” that works in low internet rural environments. The goal is to allow farmers to sell crops directly to retailers without middlemen. The interface should be simple, clean, and farmer-friendly with large buttons and clear icons.

🎨 Design Style

Agriculture theme with green, brown, and earthy colors

Large buttons for easy use

Minimal text and clear icons

Simple navigation for users with low digital literacy

Mobile-first design

Offline-first indicator
